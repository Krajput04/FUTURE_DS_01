üìà Key Findings              
Market Dominance: The business is heavily reliant on the UK market, representing the vast majority of customers.             
Seasonality: There is a significant surge in sales during the Q4 period (November/December), likely driven by holiday shopping.

                      suggesting an opportunity for international expansion.
